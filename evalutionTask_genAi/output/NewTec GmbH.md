# NewTec GmbH

**Hiring fit:** Yes - strong safety/security embedded engineering employer in southern Germany with real leadership scope.

## Applicant overview

- **Locations/departments:** Headquarters in Pfaffenhofen an der Roth near Neu-Ulm, with sites in Ulm-Soflingen, Bremen, Freiburg, Mannheim, and Friedrichshafen. Departments include safety engineering, embedded software/hardware, cybersecurity, testing, medical, automotive, avionics, rail, industrial, training, and consulting.
- **Revenue/growth:** Revenue is not public. NewTec reports around **300** employees at six locations and more than three decades of operation since 1986.
- **Market/future:** Functional safety, embedded security, safe AI, medical/rail/automotive certification, and secure product lifecycle work are strong future markets.
- **Employee satisfaction:** Public career material emphasizes values, growth, safety mission, and engineering excellence. Applicants should still check consulting utilization, travel, remote policy, and manager salary band.
- **Employees:** Around **300** current employees; prior two-year counts not public.
- **Similar companies:** ITK Engineering, Vector Informatik, Hitex, BTC Embedded Systems, itemis.
