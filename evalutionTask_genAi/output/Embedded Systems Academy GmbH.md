# Embedded Systems Academy GmbH

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - deep CANopen niche, but likely too small and opaque for most hiring searches.

## Company snapshot

Embedded Systems Academy GmbH is based in Barsinghausen, Germany. It focuses on CANopen, CANopen FD, J1939, CAN bus tools, training, consulting, online courses, protocol stacks, CANopen Architect, CANopen Magic, CANopenIA, CiA447 tools, and related embedded communication products.

For embedded applicants, the technical fit is specialized but real: CAN/CANopen protocol work, industrial control, device configuration, embedded security, tools, protocol stacks, diagnostics, and training.

## Size, revenue, and growth

Public employee and revenue data is limited. The company appears small and specialist, with Embedded Systems Academy GmbH in Germany and Embedded Systems Academy Inc. in the United States. It has been active in CANopen products and education for many years.
