# DLogic EUROPE GmbH

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - relevant rugged touch-display products, but very small footprint and almost no independent employer data.

## Company snapshot

DLogic EUROPE GmbH is a German supplier/manufacturer of industrial-grade touch display computers and rugged HMI systems. The company is based in Jestetten/Altenburg, Germany, and lists Georg Paul Israel and Urs Boesiger as managing directors.

Its products include rugged touch display computers, outdoor/high-brightness displays, optical bonding, safety-glass options, ARM- and Intel-based display computers, stainless-steel housings, custom display modules, software board-support enhancements, UI/application development, and manufacturing/support services. Target applications include e-charging stations, self-service terminals, automotive, marine, industrial, medical, vending, laboratory, harsh environments, and digital signage.
