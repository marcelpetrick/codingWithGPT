# Tessolve Semiconductor GmbH

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - strong semiconductor learning platform, but watch workload and pay.

## Applicant Summary

Tessolve is a semiconductor engineering and turnkey solutions provider with services across pre-silicon, post-silicon, silicon bring-up, ASIC design, VLSI, test engineering, embedded systems, hardware/software product engineering, 5G, mmWave, RF, PCB design, automotive, avionics, industrial, and medical domains. Tessolve Semiconductor GmbH is the German presence of this global company. For applicants seeking semiconductor test/validation and embedded engineering exposure, Tessolve can be a strong learning platform.

## Location And Departments

- Global headquarters: Bangalore, Karnataka, India.
- German presence: Tessolve Semiconductor GmbH, with global offices also in the US, Singapore, Malaysia, UK, Canada, Japan, Taiwan, Philippines, and test labs in India, Singapore, Malaysia, Austin, and San Jose.
- Departments/work areas: pre-silicon, post-silicon validation, turnkey ASIC, VLSI design, ATE/test engineering, RF, board design, embedded hardware/software, product engineering, 5G/mmWave, silicon photonics, HBM/HPI, automotive, avionics, industrial, medical.
- Remote/mobile work: careers page mentions work-from-home and flexible working hours when life or work demands it; real availability likely depends on project/lab/client needs.

## Revenue And Growth

Tessolve is privately held and does not publish revenue in the sources found.

- Founded in 2004.
- LinkedIn lists 1,001-5,000 employees and states **3,500+** employees worldwide.
- Tessolve says its clientele includes Tier 1 clients across market segments and 9 of the top 10 semiconductor companies.
- A 2025 company recap describes strategic growth, expanded engineering capabilities, and demand for integrated chip design, validation, embedded software, testing, and product engineering.

Interpretation: active growth in a strong semiconductor services market, but exact financials are not public.

## Market And Goals

The market is strong: semiconductor design, validation, test engineering, ASICs, embedded AI, 5G/mmWave, automotive electronics, and edge systems all have durable demand. Tessolve’s value proposition is end-to-end semiconductor and embedded engineering support from specification to product.

## Employee Satisfaction

- Glassdoor: 3.8 from about 610 reviews; 73-75% recommendation; around 89-90% CEO approval; high positive business outlook.
- Positive themes: good learning curve, semiconductor test/validation expertise, supportive culture, career opportunities, good for freshers and early-career engineers.
- Negative themes: below-market pay in some roles, slow growth/raises, long working hours, client-work pressure, and benefits not always competitive.
- Pay and salary increases: moderate to weak signal; compensation/benefits typically around 3.4-3.5.
- Ambitious people: good for learning and semiconductor project exposure, especially test/validation; less ideal if the applicant prioritizes high pay or predictable work-life balance.
- Family friendliness: mixed because some flexible-work benefits exist, but workload concerns appear in reviews.

## Decision

Good company for hiring? **Maybe.** Tessolve is a good target for semiconductor test, validation, ASIC, and embedded engineers who want fast learning and global projects. I would negotiate salary carefully and validate expected hours, client assignment, German team structure, travel/lab needs, and career progression before accepting.
