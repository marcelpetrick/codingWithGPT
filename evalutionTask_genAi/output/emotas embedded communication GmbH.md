# emotas embedded communication GmbH

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - excellent industrial communication niche, but very small company and limited employer-review evidence.

## Company snapshot

emotas embedded communication GmbH is an embedded software development company based in Merseburg, Germany. It develops protocol stacks, tools, training, consulting, integration support, and maintenance for industrial communication protocols.

Its focus areas include CAN, CANopen, CANopen FD, J1939, UDS, EnergyBus, and EtherCAT. The company is active in CAN in Automation standardization, which is a good technical credibility signal.
