# Avnet Abacus Avnet EMG GmbH

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - strong global distributor, but cyclical market and mixed German employer reviews.

## Applicant Summary

Avnet Abacus is part of Avnet, a global electronic-components distributor and solutions provider. Avnet Abacus focuses on IP&E, power, battery, smart connectivity, and component distribution from idea to production. For applicants in embedded distribution, FAE/design-in, supply chain, account management, product management, and electronics sales, Avnet offers scale and broad supplier exposure. The main caution is mixed German review data, especially around communication and career development.

## Location And Departments

- Avnet global headquarters: Phoenix, Arizona, USA.
- Avnet Abacus headquarters listed by LinkedIn: Diegem, Belgium.
- German Avnet footprint includes Poing and other German locations in kununu data; Avnet EMG GmbH is the local German operating context.
- Departments/work areas: electronic components, embedded solutions, IP&E/power/battery products, design-chain services, supply-chain services, IoT, product marketing, sales, FAE/design-in, logistics, and customer/supplier management.
- Remote/mobile work: German reviews mention good home-office equipment in some cases; policy likely varies by role and manager.

## Revenue And Growth

Avnet is public and publishes financials.

- Fiscal 2025: revenue of USD 22.2B, down 6.55% from 2024.
- Fiscal 2024: revenue of USD 23.8B, down 10.48% from 2023.
- Fiscal 2023: revenue of USD 26.5B, up 9.16% from 2022.
- Late 2025/early 2026 signals show some recovery: Avnet reported Q4 FY2025 sales at USD 5.6B, and Macrotrends shows the Dec. 2025 quarter up 11.58% year over year.

Interpretation: large and resilient, but exposed to semiconductor-cycle swings.

## Market And Goals

Avnet operates in a strong but cyclical market: electronics distribution, embedded systems, IoT, design-chain support, and supply-chain services. Long-term demand for electronics remains strong, but short-to-midterm conditions depend heavily on inventory correction, customer demand, and semiconductor cycles.

## Employee Satisfaction

- kununu Avnet Germany: 3.3 score from 173 reviews; 53% recommendation in the last two years.
- kununu Avnet Embedded: 3.1 score from 129 reviews; 67% recommendation in the last two years, but lower scores for career/development and communication.
- Salary: kununu Avnet Germany shows 54% salary satisfaction and 3.4 salary/benefits, slightly above trade-sector average; comments are mixed.
- Positive themes: stable company, good colleagues, good equipment/work conditions, market-relevant customer/supplier exposure.
- Negative themes: career/development weakness, communication issues, traditional culture, and variable management.
- Ambitious people: good for broad electronics-market exposure and commercial/FAE career paths; less ideal if fast promotions and modern startup-style culture are priorities.
