# MEV Elektronik Service GmbH

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - very good employer reviews for an electronics distributor, but software engineering management fit is limited.

## Applicant overview

- **Locations/departments:** German specialist distributor/manufacturer representative for electronic components, modules, and systems serving Germany and Central/Eastern Europe. Departments include FAE/application engineering, sales, logistics, supplier management, lab/demo support, power, display technology, and optoelectronics.
- **Revenue/growth:** Revenue is not public. The company positions itself as a leading specialist distributor with 15 engineers supporting customers.
- **Market/future:** Design-in distribution for power, display, optoelectronics, embedded modules, and logistics support is resilient, but not software-led.
- **Employee satisfaction:** Kununu shows 4.2/5 from 21 reviews, 100% recent recommendation, strong pay/benefits at 4.7, good leadership, work-life balance, and interesting tasks. Application engineer salary datapoints are about EUR 82.7k.
- **Employees:** Public current/prior counts not reliable; MEV states **15** engineers in technical support.
- **Similar companies:** EBV Elektronik, Endrich, Ineltek, Texim Europe, Rutronik.
