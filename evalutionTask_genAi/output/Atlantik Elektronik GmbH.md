# Atlantik Elektronik GmbH

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - established semiconductor distributor with solid market fit, but mixed parent-group culture reviews.

## Applicant Summary

Atlantik Elektronik GmbH is a Planegg-based semiconductor distributor and design-in solution provider founded in 1977. It supports customers with components, technical concepts, project planning, custom hardware/software, logistics, and production support. It is part of Atlantik Networxx AG. For applicants in semiconductor sales, FAE/design-in, embedded IoT, edge AI, wireless, displays, sensors, and hardware/software integration, the work looks relevant and market-facing.

The market is attractive but competitive: semiconductor distribution, embedded IoT, edge AI, wireless connectivity, robotics, and industrial embedded platforms. Atlantik's stated goal is to bring and keep customers in a market-leading position through time-to-technology, time-to-market, and reduced total cost of ownership.

## Employee Satisfaction

- Atlantik Networxx kununu: 3.7 score from 47 employee reviews, 68% recommendation.
- Positive themes: salary/company car/benefits in some reviews, technology-oriented work, and established customer/supplier ecosystem.
- Negative themes: recent review complaints about control, lack of trust, limited appreciation, unequal treatment around development/home office, and management behavior.
- Atlantik Elektronik-specific Glassdoor/kununu data is limited; parent-group data is the best available culture proxy.
- Ambitious people: potentially good for FAE/design-in and customer-facing technology roles; less certain for long-term career development until manager and team are validated.
