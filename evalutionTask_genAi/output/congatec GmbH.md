# congatec GmbH

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - excellent embedded computing market fit, but employer reviews raise leadership, salary, and working-condition concerns.

## Company snapshot

congatec GmbH is a Deggendorf-based embedded computing company founded in 2004/2005. It provides computer-on-modules and embedded/edge computing building blocks, including COM-HPC, COM Express, SMARC, Qseven, single-board computers, rugged industrial computing, virtualization, IoT software, and edge AI platforms.

For embedded applicants, the technical relevance is very strong: industrial automation, robotics, medical technology, telecom, edge computing, real-time consolidation, hypervisors, secure IIoT, and long-lifecycle hardware platforms.

## Work culture and applicant signal

The employer signal is mixed. Kununu shows about **3.6/5** with **58% recommendation** over the last two years. Positive themes include colleague cohesion, equality, interesting technical products, and a relevant market. Negative themes include salary satisfaction, working conditions, leadership style, career development, and office/building issues. Glassdoor is a smaller sample but also mixed, with low recommendation and senior-management scores.

This is worth considering for engineers who prioritize embedded hardware/edge computing experience and can verify the team locally. It should not be accepted blindly: applicants should check manager quality, salary band, remote policy, office conditions, decision culture, and turnover in the target team.

## Market outlook

The market outlook is good. Industrial edge computing, AI at the edge, COM-HPC, medical/robotics platforms, virtualization, secure remote management, and long-lifecycle embedded modules are important growth areas. Product-market fit is stronger than the employer-review signal.
