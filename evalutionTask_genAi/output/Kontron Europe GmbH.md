# Kontron Europe GmbH

**Hiring fit:** Yes - major Bavaria-rooted embedded computing employer with real software/IoT management scope, though hardware-cycle pressure should be checked.

## Applicant overview

- **Locations/departments:** Kontron Europe is the largest subsidiary of Kontron AG and includes Augsburg/Ismaning-era roots plus European operations. Departments include embedded boards, COMs, industrial PCs, IoT gateways, software, security, connectivity, production, sales, support, and custom solutions.
- **Revenue/growth:** Kontron Europe itself says it has about **800** employees. Group-level revenue should be reviewed in Kontron AG reports; the Europe subsidiary is part of a listed embedded/IoT group.
- **Market/future:** Edge computing, industrial IoT, embedded platforms, secure connectivity, transportation, medical, automation, and software-defined edge infrastructure are relevant future markets.
- **Employee satisfaction:** Employer signal varies by group/site; applicants should check local team reviews, restructuring history, remote policy, and whether the role is product software or customer solution delivery.
- **Employees:** Kontron Europe reports about **800** employees; previous two-year subsidiary counts not found.
- **Similar companies:** Advantech, congatec, DATA MODUL, FORTEC, BRESSNER Technology.
