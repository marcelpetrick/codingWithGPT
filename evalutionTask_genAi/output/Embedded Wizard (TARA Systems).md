# Embedded Wizard (TARA Systems)

**Hiring fit:** Yes - Munich-based embedded GUI software specialist with a strong product niche, though company size is modest.

## Company snapshot

Embedded Wizard is an embedded graphical user interface tool created by TARA Systems GmbH in Munich. TARA Systems develops embedded GUI technology and digital TV software/components. Embedded Wizard is used to build high-performance GUIs for embedded systems across consumer electronics, industrial, automotive, and mobile devices.

For embedded software applicants, this is highly relevant: GUI frameworks, HMI tools, embedded graphics, STM32 and MCU/MPU ecosystems, customer SDKs, performance optimization, tooling, and product engineering.

## Size, revenue, and growth

The Embedded Wizard LinkedIn page lists TARA Systems as a company with **more than 30 employees** and focused since 1990 on GUI tools and digital TV reception solutions. Public revenue is not disclosed.

## Work culture and applicant signal

Independent employer-review data is limited, but the product signal is strong. Embedded Wizard won first place in the Tools category at the Embedded Award 2024, and TARA Systems has a long collaboration with STMicroelectronics. The company is also located in Munich, which is relevant for local applicants.

This is attractive for engineers and managers who want a real embedded software product rather than pure services. The caveat is company scale: management scope may be smaller than at Vector, dSPACE, Siemens, or Synopsys.

## Market outlook

The outlook is good. Embedded products increasingly need modern GUIs on constrained hardware, and embedded graphics tooling remains relevant across industrial, medical, consumer, appliance, and automotive devices.
