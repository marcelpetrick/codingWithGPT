# Elevake GmbH - EPCB24

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - practical PCB assembly and elevator electronics niche, but very small and too little employer data.

## Company snapshot

Elevake GmbH / EPCB24 is based in Monheim am Rhein, Germany. The company presents two related activities: Elevake focuses on elevator electronics such as CANopen, industrial displays, TFT displays, hall lanterns, elevator buttons, and protocol-compatible elevator components; EPCB24 focuses on SMT assembly for prototypes and small batches from German production.

For embedded applicants, this is more hardware/manufacturing oriented than software-heavy: PCB assembly, small-series electronics, displays, elevator interfaces, CANopen-related systems, and customer-specific electronics.
