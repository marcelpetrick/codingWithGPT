# ITK Engineering GmbH

**Hiring fit:** Yes - strong engineering-services employer with Bavaria/Munich access, good reviews, and real software leadership opportunities.

## Applicant overview

- **Locations/departments:** Headquarters in Rulzheim with multiple German locations and Bosch ownership. Departments cover software, embedded systems, systems engineering, AI, medical, automotive, rail, industrial, robotics, safety, cybersecurity, testing, project management, and consulting.
- **Revenue/growth:** Separate revenue is not public in this quick pass because ITK is part of Bosch. The employer scale is substantial and stable, with hundreds of reviews and many salary datapoints.
- **Market/future:** Software-defined vehicles, safety-critical systems, medical software, automation, AI, and embedded engineering services are relevant markets. Consulting exposure and project staffing are the main risks.
- **Employee satisfaction:** Kununu shows 4.1/5 from 386 reviews, 65% recent recommendation, strong work atmosphere, work-life balance, collegiality, and pay satisfaction of 72%. Software developer average salary is about EUR 68k; team lead average around EUR 105.5k.
- **Employees:** Public precise current/prior counts not extracted; LinkedIn and Bosch context indicate a large engineering-services company.
- **Similar companies:** EDAG, Bertrandt, Alten, KPIT, Vector Informatik.
