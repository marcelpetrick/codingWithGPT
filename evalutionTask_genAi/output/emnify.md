# emnify

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - strong cloud-native IoT connectivity business, but reviews show communication and stability concerns.

## Company snapshot

emnify is a Berlin-based IoT connectivity company founded in 2014. It provides cellular IoT connectivity, network access, device management, and cloud-based infrastructure for connected products and IoT deployments at scale.

For software applicants, emnify is relevant for cloud engineering, telecom infrastructure, distributed systems, APIs, IoT connectivity, platform operations, network access, observability, and customer-facing IoT services. It is less embedded-hardware-centric than many other companies in this list.

## Work culture and applicant signal

Employer reviews are mixed. Kununu shows about **3.6/5** from **44 reviews**. Glassdoor shows around **3.4/5** with **61% recommendation**. Positive signals include interesting tasks, remote work, flexible hours, office benefits, IT equipment, and IoT projects. Negative signals include communication, leadership, transparency, and some references to hire-and-fire style instability.

This can be attractive for engineers who want a modern SaaS/telecom IoT platform rather than classic embedded firmware. It is not a clear “Yes” because applicants should verify team stability, leadership quality, product direction, and whether the role has real engineering ownership.

## Market outlook

The IoT connectivity market remains relevant, especially for global deployments, eSIM, private networks, device operations, and connected products. Competition is significant from telecoms, hyperscalers, and specialist IoT connectivity platforms.
