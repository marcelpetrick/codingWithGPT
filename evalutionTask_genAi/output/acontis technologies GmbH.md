# acontis technologies GmbH

**Hiring fit:** Yes - strong niche market and very positive culture signals, with small-company caveats.

## Applicant Summary

acontis technologies GmbH is a Weingarten-based embedded software company focused on EtherCAT, Windows real-time software, and real-time virtualization/hypervisor technology for industrial controllers. It looks like a technically focused specialist with a strong position in industrial automation. For applicants interested in embedded real-time systems, industrial communication, EtherCAT, robotics, and automation, this is a strong target.

The company operates in attractive embedded/industrial markets: EtherCAT, real-time control, robotics, semiconductor manufacturing, medical/surgical robotics, automotive, aerospace/defense, and industrial automation. These markets have durable demand because industrial systems increasingly need deterministic communication, mixed-criticality compute, and virtualization.
