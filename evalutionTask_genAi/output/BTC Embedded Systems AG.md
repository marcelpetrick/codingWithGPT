# BTC Embedded Systems AG

**Hiring fit:** Yes - excellent embedded safety tooling niche, strong automotive relevance, and very strong German employer reviews.

## Company snapshot

BTC Embedded Systems AG is an Oldenburg-based German software company founded in 1999. It builds automated test, verification, and validation tools for safety-critical embedded software, especially model-based automotive development with Simulink, TargetLink, Embedded Coder, AUTOSAR, C/C++ production code, and processor-in-the-loop workflows.

Its main products include BTC EmbeddedPlatform, EmbeddedTester, EmbeddedSpecifier, and EmbeddedValidator. The tooling is positioned around requirements-based testing, back-to-back testing, regression testing, code coverage, formal specification, formal verification, continuous integration, and ISO 26262-compliant development.

## Size, revenue, and growth

BTC states that it has **over 200 employees** and global customer usage among car makers and suppliers. Public standalone revenue data is not readily disclosed. ## Work culture and applicant signal

The employer signal is strong. Kununu shows around **4.6/5** with **100% recommendation** over the last two years. Category scores are high for work-life balance, atmosphere, communication, leadership, colleague cohesion, interesting tasks, and career/salary. BTC also advertises flexible work, personal development, 30 days of vacation, company pension support, Hansefit, bike leasing, and thesis supervision.

For an ambitious embedded/software engineer, this looks like a very good fit if the applicant likes model-based development, automotive standards, test automation, formal methods, and safety-critical engineering. The work is likely more methodical and quality-process-heavy than fast consumer product work, but that is exactly the domain advantage.

## Market outlook

The outlook is strong. Software-defined vehicles, ADAS, electric powertrains, ISO 26262, AUTOSAR, and rising verification complexity all increase demand for automated safety testing and formal verification. BTC is in a specialized tooling segment with high customer switching costs.
