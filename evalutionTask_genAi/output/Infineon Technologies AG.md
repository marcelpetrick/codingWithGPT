# Infineon Technologies AG

**Hiring fit:** Yes - top-tier Munich semiconductor employer with strong software/embedded management scope, despite current semiconductor-cycle pressure.

## Applicant overview

- **Locations/departments:** Headquarters in Neubiberg near Munich, with major German and global sites. Departments include automotive, power systems, IoT, microcontrollers, software, security, AI/data, manufacturing, quality, sales, corporate R&D, and operations.
- **Revenue/growth:** FY 2025 revenue was **EUR 14.662bn**, down 2% from about **EUR 15.0bn** in FY 2024; FY 2026 outlook is moderate growth. Q4 FY 2025 revenue was EUR 3.943bn and AI power revenue targets were raised.
- **Market/future:** Power semiconductors, automotive MCUs, AI data-center power, industrial IoT, security chips, and decarbonization are excellent future markets. Automotive weakness is the main cyclical risk.
- **Employee satisfaction:** Germany Kununu/Glassdoor should be checked by role and site, but Infineon is a large, mature employer with real leadership tracks, Munich proximity, and strong product impact. Watch bureaucracy, matrix complexity, and cycle-related cost pressure.
- **Employees:** **57,077** in FY 2025, **58,065** in FY 2024, **58,590** in FY 2023.
- **Similar companies:** NXP, STMicroelectronics, Bosch, Renesas, Texas Instruments.
