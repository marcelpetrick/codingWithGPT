# esd electronics gmbh

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - strong industrial communication technology, but small employer-review sample is mixed.

## Company snapshot

esd electronics gmbh is an internationally active, medium-sized company based in Hannover. It develops and manufactures hardware and software for industrial automation, with a focus on customer-specific solutions.

Its work includes PowerPC, ARM, Intel, 8/16-bit microcontroller systems, fieldbus systems such as CAN/CANopen/CAN FD, PROFIBUS, PROFINET, EtherCAT, wireless coupling, AMC, CompactPCI, PMC, PCI, PCIe, VMEbus, XMC, USB devices, gateways, I/O modules, industrial PCs, and custom electronics for automotive, mechanical engineering, medical technology, and aerospace.

## Work culture and applicant signal

Kununu data is sparse: about **3.5/5** from **3 reviews**, with positive comments around atmosphere, family-like colleague relationships, and social responsibility, but concerns about investment in the future and management reliability. This is not enough to make a strong “Yes” call.

The company is technically attractive for embedded communication, industrial automation, CAN/EtherCAT, and hardware/software engineers. Applicants should verify modernization, development process, salary, leadership, remote work, and whether the role has real software leadership scope.

## Market outlook

The market is stable. CAN, EtherCAT, PROFINET, PROFIBUS, industrial gateways, rugged electronics, and custom automation hardware remain relevant. Growth may be steady rather than explosive.
