# CELUS GmbH

**Hiring fit:** Yes - strong electronics-design automation niche with good growth signals, with normal startup-stage risk.

## Company snapshot

CELUS GmbH is a Munich-based deep-tech software company founded in 2018. It builds the CELUS Design Platform, an AI-assisted electronics design tool that helps engineers convert functional requirements into schematics, bills of materials, component selections, and reusable design blocks. CELUS positions its CUBOs as digital datasheets that replace static PDF datasheets and application notes with structured, design-ready information.

For embedded and electronics applicants, this is a relevant bridge between EDA, PCB design, component engineering, AI, SaaS, and hardware workflows. The company has locations in Munich, Porto, and Austin.

## Work culture and applicant signal

The employer signal is positive but not as broad as for larger firms. CELUS publicly highlights growth, international locations, and a mission to make electronics engineering faster and more accessible. Recent LinkedIn posts also mention a repeated kununu Top Company award, which is a favorable signal, although applicants should still read current reviews directly before accepting.

This looks attractive for engineers who want high ownership, product impact, AI-enabled engineering tools, and exposure to both software and electronics. The main risks are startup execution risk, shifting priorities, and potentially less predictable processes than in established automotive or semiconductor companies.

## Market outlook

The market outlook is good. Electronics design remains slow and fragmented, component selection is painful, and AI-assisted hardware engineering is an emerging category. CELUS also sits in a useful ecosystem position between component manufacturers, distributors, EDA vendors, and design engineers.
