# AST-X GmbH

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - technically attractive embedded/IoT work, but limited public culture and financial data.

## Applicant Summary

AST-X GmbH is a Forchheim-based hardware and software development company focused on embedded systems, industrial IoT, LPWAN gateways, sensor technology, and product industrialization from prototype to series production. It appears technically relevant for embedded engineers, hardware developers, and IoT specialists. The main limitation for applicants is transparency: it is small, private, and has little public employer-review data.

## Location And Departments

- Headquarters: Breitweidig 9, 91301 Forchheim, Germany.
- Departments/work areas: embedded software, hardware development, PCB/layout, mechanics, qualification/documentation, industrial manufacturing, IoT gateways, LPWAN/mioty/LoRaWAN, sensor systems, project engineering.
- Remote/mobile work: a LinkedIn hiring post mentions flexible working hours and home-office option for an embedded senior software role; exact policy likely depends on role and hardware lab needs.

## Revenue And Growth

AST-X is private and does not publish revenue.

- Founded in 2014.
- LinkedIn lists 11-50 employees and about **10** visible employee profiles.
- A company-profile source lists **18** employees as of 2021.
- Official website claims experience from more than 900 projects across different industries.
- Current activity includes embedded world 2026, mioty ecosystem participation, product promotion for VIORYTI GATE AX2, and open roles.

Interpretation: stable small engineering/product company with active market presence; revenue growth cannot be verified.

## Market And Goals

AST-X works in promising embedded and industrial markets: IoT, smart infrastructure, LPWAN connectivity, energy/electrical industry, machine and plant engineering, smart city, and sensor-based industrial monitoring. The VIORYTI gateway positioning is relevant because scalable low-power industrial connectivity is a real demand area.
