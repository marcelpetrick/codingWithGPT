# DISPLAY VISIONS GmbH

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - solid small industrial-display specialist, but independent employer data is too thin for a strong recommendation.

## Company snapshot

DISPLAY VISIONS GmbH, formerly Electronic Assembly, is a German display and HMI manufacturer based in Gilching near Munich. The company was founded in 1977 and develops, produces, and sells LCD, TFT, OLED, touch, intelligent display, Modbus HMI, and customized display/control-panel solutions for industrial, medical, automation, machinery, and IT applications.

For embedded applicants, the technical relevance is moderate to good: HMI units, display modules, touch panels, embedded display controllers, industrial interfaces, PLC/Modbus integration, and long-lifecycle display products.

## Work culture and applicant signal

Independent employer-review data is limited. A similarly named Kununu profile has only a very small sample, so it is not enough to make a confident culture assessment. Public hiring posts show active technical sales hiring, and the company emphasizes long-serving qualified employees, in-house production, development, support, and training.

This could be a good fit for someone who wants hands-on industrial display/HMI work in a small manufacturer. It is less compelling for someone seeking a large engineering organization, broad career ladder, or deep software/firmware complexity. Applicants should verify salary, role scope, remote flexibility, development process, and whether engineering work is internal product development or mostly customer adaptation.

## Market outlook

Industrial HMI and display modules are stable markets, especially where long availability, support, ruggedness, medical/industrial quality, and short delivery times matter. Growth is likely steady and niche rather than high-growth software scale-up style.
