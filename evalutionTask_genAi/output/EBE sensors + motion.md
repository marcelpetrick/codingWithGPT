# EBE sensors + motion

**Hiring fit:** Yes - stable sensor and mechatronics manufacturer with explicit flexibility, benefits, and long-tenure signals.

## Company snapshot

EBE sensors + motion is the brand of EBE Elektro-Bau-Elemente GmbH, based in Leinfelden-Echterdingen, Germany. The company develops and manufactures sensor technologies, HMI components, electromechanical components, and mechatronic solutions for OEMs and series producers.

Its products include fluid sensors, touch sensors, position sensors, strain sensors, encoders, rotary switches, sensor buttons, solenoids, and mechatronics for industrial applications, medical technology, appliances, and mobility.

## Work culture and applicant signal

The company’s own career page gives unusually concrete employer information: flexible and family-conscious working hours, mobile office where possible, part-time options, open and transparent leadership, annual employee reviews, training, profit-sharing bonus after probation, regional shopping allowance, corporate benefits, pension options, JobRad, Deutschlandticket support, private and work accident insurance, parking, canteen lunch, health day, company events, and extra paid child sick days.

It also states an average employee tenure of **8.84 years**, which is a good stability signal. Independent review depth still needs checking before accepting an offer, but the visible employer package is stronger than for many small hardware manufacturers.

## Market outlook

The market is stable. Sensors, HMI components, mechatronics, medical devices, industrial equipment, appliances, and mobility all need reliable OEM components. Growth is likely steady and product/customer driven.
