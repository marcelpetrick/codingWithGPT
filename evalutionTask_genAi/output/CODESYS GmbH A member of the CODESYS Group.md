# CODESYS GmbH A member of the CODESYS Group

**Hiring fit:** Yes - leading industrial automation software niche with excellent German employer reviews.

## Company snapshot

CODESYS Group, headquartered in Kempten, Germany, develops CODESYS, a hardware-independent automation software suite based on IEC 61131-3. The product family includes the CODESYS Development System, CODESYS Control runtime, CODESYS Automation Server, visualization, fieldbus and real-time Ethernet integration, motion/CNC/robotics functions, cloud/IIoT connectivity, and certified safety products.

For embedded and automation applicants, this is highly relevant: PLC runtimes, compilers, industrial protocols, safety, real-time systems, edge controllers, cloud-connected automation, visualization, and tools used by hundreds of automation device manufacturers.
