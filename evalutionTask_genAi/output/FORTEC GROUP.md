# FORTEC GROUP

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - financially established Munich-area electronics group with good reviews, but recent revenue contraction and limited pure software scope.

## Applicant overview

- **Locations/departments:** Headquarters in Germering near Munich. The group covers displays, embedded systems, power supplies, development, production, sales, support, finance, and corporate functions, with international subsidiaries.
- **Revenue/growth:** FY 2025 revenue was about **EUR 79.7m**, down from **EUR 94.5m** in FY 2024 and **EUR 105.9m** in FY 2023. This is a stable listed company, but not currently a growth story.
- **Market/future:** Embedded systems, industrial displays, power supplies, medical and industrial electronics are durable markets. Software management roles may exist around embedded/display systems but are likely limited in number.
- **Employee satisfaction:** Kununu shows 4.0/5 from a small sample, 100% recent recommendation, good collegiality, work-life balance, home office, flexible hours, and salary satisfaction. Sample size is only eight reviews.
- **Employees:** **242** at Dec. 2025, **239** at Jun. 2025, **246** at Jun. 2024.
- **Similar companies:** DATA MODUL, Kontron, BRESSNER Technology, Advantech, NEXCOM.
