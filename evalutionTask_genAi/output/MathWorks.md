# MathWorks

**Hiring fit:** Yes - excellent engineering-software employer with strong culture and Germany signal, though remote flexibility and career speed can be limited.

## Applicant overview

- **Locations/departments:** Global headquarters in Natick, Massachusetts; Germany has local sales/application engineering presence. Departments include MATLAB/Simulink engineering, code generation, embedded/autonomous systems, verification, AI, customer engineering, sales, support, product, and corporate functions.
- **Revenue/growth:** Private company; revenue is not formally public. The company is large, profitable-looking, and longstanding, with thousands of global employees and dominant engineering-software products.
- **Market/future:** Model-based design, simulation, embedded code generation, controls, AI, autonomous systems, safety verification, and digital engineering remain strong markets.
- **Employee satisfaction:** Glassdoor global shows 4.3/5 from about 2,500+ reviews, 88% recommendation, 94% CEO approval, and strong work-life/culture. Germany reviews show 4.6/5 from a small sample, with excellent culture/work-life but lower career opportunity and compensation scores; recent comments cite limited remote flexibility.
- **Employees:** Public exact current/prior counts not extracted; Glassdoor review scale suggests a large global employer.
- **Similar companies:** Siemens EDA, Ansys, Dassault Systemes, NI/Emerson, Synopsys.
