# GitLab

**Hiring fit:** Yes - strong remote-first software platform company with excellent compensation signals, but management culture should be checked carefully.

## Applicant overview

- **Locations/departments:** All-remote company with German entity presence. Departments include engineering, product, security, support, sales, customer success, marketing, finance, legal, and people operations.
- **Revenue/growth:** FY 2025 revenue grew 29% year over year. Q2 FY 2026 revenue was **USD 236.0m**, also up 29% year over year. The company remains growth-oriented, with AI-native DevSecOps as the current theme.
- **Market/future:** DevSecOps, AI-assisted software delivery, compliance, and platform engineering are strong future markets. GitLab has a credible enterprise product platform.
- **Employee satisfaction:** Kununu Germany shows 3.3/5 from eight reviews, 75% recent recommendation, 100% home office/flexible-hours benefit confirmation, and strong pay/benefits at 4.5/5. Risk signal: regional/European management criticism.
- **Employees:** Public current exact employee count not extracted in this pass; GitLab is a large public software company with global all-remote hiring.
- **Similar companies:** GitHub, Atlassian, JFrog, CircleCI, Harness.
