# E4You e.V.

**Hiring fit:** No - useful embedded network, but not a normal direct employer for applicants.

## Company snapshot

E4You e.V., also known as Embedded4You, is a Munich-area association and competence network for embedded technologies. It was founded in 2007 and brings together more than 30 small and medium-sized companies from German-speaking countries. The network pools skills in embedded software, software design, test and verification, secure embedded systems, IoT platforms, automotive electronics, aerospace, industrial controls, mechanical engineering, and security.

This is better understood as a network and industry association than as a normal hiring target. The practical hiring opportunity is usually through member companies such as Razorcat Development GmbH, PROTOS Software GmbH, or other E4Y members.

## Size, revenue, and growth

E4You says its member network includes **30+ SMEs** with **well over 2,000 engineers and specialists**. That is network capacity, not direct headcount. Revenue and direct employee data for the association are not meaningfully comparable to operating companies.

## Work culture and applicant signal

There is no useful independent employer-review signal for E4You as a direct employer. For applicants, the right move is to use E4You as a map of embedded SMEs, not as a primary job application target.

The hiring fit is therefore negative as a direct employer, but positive as a discovery source: member firms may be excellent embedded employers depending on their specialization, culture, and open roles.

## Market outlook

The network serves a durable market: embedded systems, secure software, IoT, automotive electronics, aerospace, and industrial automation. The association itself benefits when German-speaking embedded SMEs need shared visibility and cooperative delivery capacity.
