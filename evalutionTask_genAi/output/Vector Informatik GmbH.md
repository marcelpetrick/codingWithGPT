# Vector Informatik GmbH

**Hiring fit:** Yes - strong embedded automotive software company with excellent employer reviews.

## Applicant Summary

Vector Informatik GmbH is a Stuttgart-based software and tooling company for embedded systems and automotive electronics. It provides tools, software components, hardware, and services for ECU development, diagnostics, testing, calibration, AUTOSAR, automotive Ethernet, cyber security, ADAS, V2X, and software-defined vehicles. For applicants in embedded software, automotive systems, tooling, testing, diagnostics, and systems engineering, Vector is one of the strongest targets in this dataset.

- Current activity shows continued investment in software-defined vehicle tooling, AI-assisted engineering workflows, PREEvision systems engineering, and Vector/QNX foundational vehicle software platform work.

Interpretation: financially opaque but very established, deeply embedded in automotive development workflows, and highly relevant to the SDV transition.

## Market And Goals

Vector operates in a strong, specialized future market: software-defined vehicles, automotive electronics, ECU testing, diagnostics, cyber security, AUTOSAR, Ethernet, and embedded development infrastructure. The automotive sector is under pressure, but software complexity is increasing, which supports demand for Vector-style tooling and software platforms.
