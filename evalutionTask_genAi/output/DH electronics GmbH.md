# DH electronics GmbH

**Hiring fit:** Yes - focused embedded systems work with positive small-company employer signals and good flexibility benefits.

## Company snapshot

DH electronics GmbH is a Bergen, Bavaria-based embedded systems company founded in 1991. It develops standard and customer-specific embedded systems and acts as a full-service provider from concept and development through production, logistics, and product support.

The company works on system-on-modules, single-board computers, HMIs, IoT gateways, edge computers, mainline Linux, long-term BSP maintenance, STM32/i.MX platforms, custom electronics, test benches, and secure maintainable embedded systems.

## Work culture and applicant signal

Kununu shows around **4.1/5** from a small sample. Positive signals include work-life balance, colleague cohesion, leadership, interesting tasks, home office, flexible working hours, parking, company events, pension support, JobRad/Wellpass, and repeated Top Company awards. Current career pages mention flexitime without core hours, onboarding support, training, company pension, and team events.

This looks attractive for embedded hardware/software engineers who want practical product ownership in a family-like small company. The main caveat is scale: career ladder, salary ceiling, and project variety should be checked directly during interviews.

## Market outlook

The market outlook is good. Long-lifecycle embedded systems, industrial Linux, cyber-resilience requirements, secure BSP maintenance, SoMs, and custom embedded platforms are all relevant. Small-company growth will depend on product pipeline and partner channels.
