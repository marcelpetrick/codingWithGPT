# Synopsys

**Hiring fit:** Yes - top-tier EDA and simulation employer with strong German reviews, though post-Ansys integration and restructuring are real risks.

## Company snapshot

Synopsys is a global engineering software company headquartered in Sunnyvale, California. It is one of the leaders in electronic design automation, semiconductor IP, software security, simulation, and system-level engineering tools. In July 2025 Synopsys completed its acquisition of Ansys, expanding from silicon design into a broader silicon-to-systems engineering platform.

For an embedded or semiconductor software applicant, Synopsys is highly relevant: EDA, verification, compiler/tooling workflows, semiconductor IP, automotive and aerospace systems, simulation, digital twins, AI-assisted engineering, and safety-critical product development.

## Size, revenue, and growth

Synopsys is public and financially strong. Fiscal 2025 revenue was **$7.054B**, up from **$6.127B** in fiscal 2024. Its first fiscal quarter 2026 revenue was **$2.409B**, and the company reiterated full-year fiscal 2026 revenue expectations around **$9.61B**, including expected Ansys revenue. Synopsys now describes itself as about **28,000 employees** with trailing twelve-month revenue around **$9B**.

## Work culture and applicant signal

Employer signals are generally good. Glassdoor shows about **3.9/5** with a majority recommending the company, and Kununu for Synopsys GmbH in Germany is stronger at about **4.4/5**. German category signals are particularly good for salary/benefits, image, interesting work, leadership, atmosphere, and colleague cohesion.

For hiring attractiveness, the upside is clear: excellent technical brand, strong compensation potential, global projects, and access to high-value semiconductor and simulation domains. It is likely a strong fit for ambitious engineers who want deep technical work and international career mobility.

The main risk is integration turbulence. After the Ansys acquisition, public reporting points to restructuring and layoffs during fiscal 2026. Applicants should check role stability, team ownership, manager quality, and whether the role is in a growth area such as AI-EDA, multiphysics integration, automotive systems, verification, or semiconductor IP.

## Market outlook

The market outlook is strong. AI chips, 3DIC, advanced packaging, automotive compute, aerospace, industrial systems, and digital twins all increase demand for EDA and multiphysics simulation. Synopsys now has a larger total addressable market and a strategic position across chip, package, board, and system design.
