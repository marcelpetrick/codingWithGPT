# DELTA COMPONENTS GmbH

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - relevant embedded hardware work, but the company is small and public employer-review data is very limited.

## Company snapshot

DELTA COMPONENTS GmbH is based in Waldshut-Tiengen, Germany, and was founded in 1993. It started with distribution of single-board computers and displays, then expanded into system integration, product modification, and customer-specific embedded solutions.

The company focuses on professional hardware and software solutions for automotive, mobile, railway, marine, industrial computing, video surveillance, network/GPS, digital signage, and special embedded applications. Products include fanless box PCs, monitors, panel PCs, rugged tablets, handheld devices, LCD modules, embedded PC boards, and AI/industrial computing systems.

## Work culture and applicant signal

There is too little independent employer-review data to assess satisfaction, salary progression, management quality, remote work, or benefits. That makes this a diligence-heavy hiring target.

The role could be attractive for applicants who want practical embedded hardware, rugged computing, customer-specific systems, and broad hands-on work in a small company. The risks are limited career ladder, possible dependence on a few customers or product lines, unknown salary competitiveness, and less structured people processes than larger employers.

Applicants should ask directly about project pipeline, salary band, overtime, remote work, hardware/software split, engineering ownership, and how much work is product development versus distribution/support.

## Market outlook

The market is stable for industrial, automotive, railway, marine, and rugged embedded computing. Growth is likely niche and customer-project-driven rather than venture-scale. The breadth of applications is a positive resilience signal.
