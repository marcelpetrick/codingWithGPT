# ECOS Technology GmbH

**Hiring fit:** Yes - strong industrial cybersecurity niche with very good employer-review signals.

## Company snapshot

ECOS Technology GmbH is a German software manufacturer based in Oppenheim. Founded in 1999, it specializes in IT security products for key and certificate management, secure remote access, secure remote working, secure video conferencing, PKI, identity management, and OT/IoT cybersecurity.

For embedded applicants, ECOS is especially relevant around industrial cybersecurity, machine identities, secure updates, certificate lifecycle management, OT/IIoT, automation technology, measurement/control technology, sensor systems, microtechnology, medical technology, and Cyber Resilience Act readiness.
