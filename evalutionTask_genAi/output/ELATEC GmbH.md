# ELATEC GmbH

**Hiring fit:** Yes - strong RFID/access-control niche with excellent employer reviews and solid growth signals.

## Company snapshot

ELATEC GmbH is based in Puchheim, Germany, and is part of Allegion. The company develops universal RFID, NFC, BLE, mobile-credential, and access-control reader technology for authentication and identification.

Its products are used in physical access control, secure printing, single sign-on, elevators, time and attendance, machine authentication, fleet management, EV charging, parking, vending/kiosks, smart lockers, bike/car sharing, and other secure-access applications. For embedded applicants, the work can involve firmware, RF/RFID, hardware, SDKs, mobile credentials, secure device management, and industrial/OEM integration.
