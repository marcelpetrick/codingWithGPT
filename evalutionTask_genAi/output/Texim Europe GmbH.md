# Texim Europe GmbH

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - relevant electronics-distribution niche, but employer-review evidence is thin.

## Applicant Summary

Texim Europe provides electronic components, modules, systems, engineering support, and supply-chain services for professional OEMs and system integrators. It supports markets such as industrial automation, transportation, agriculture, medical, energy, smart infrastructure, IIoT, AI, robotics, drones, displays, sensors, wireless communication, and power solutions. The German GmbH appears to be part of the wider Texim Europe footprint.

## Location And Departments

- Headquarters of Texim Europe: Haaksbergen, Netherlands.
- German locations listed by LinkedIn: Quickborn and Munich.
- Other locations: Belgium, UK/Ireland, Austria, Denmark/Nordics, Italy.
- Departments/work areas: electronic components, embedded computing, industrial displays, wireless modules, sensors, power solutions, supply-chain services, technical sales, field/application engineering, marketing, purchasing, logistics, and customer engineering support.
- Remote/mobile work: no reliable public policy found. A current marketing role in the Netherlands mentions hybrid; German role policies should be checked directly.

## Revenue And Growth

Texim Europe is private and does not publish revenue.

- Support locations in Belgium, Germany, the Netherlands, the Nordic region, and UK/Ireland.

## Market And Goals

The market is solid but competitive: embedded electronics distribution, edge AI, displays, sensors, wireless modules, and professional OEM design support. Texim’s goal is to combine technical advice, supplier network, engineering support, and supply-chain reliability to help customers future-proof electronic designs.
