# Unified Automation GmbH

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - strong OPC UA niche and positive small-sample reviews, but limited public data.

## Applicant Summary

Unified Automation GmbH is a Kalchreuth-based embedded software company specializing in OPC Unified Architecture (OPC UA) products, SDKs, tools, and services for standardized communication in automation. It serves embedded device manufacturers through enterprise application developers. For applicants interested in industrial communication, OPC UA, C/C++, C#, embedded systems, automation, and OT/IT integration, the technical niche is attractive.

## Location And Departments

- Headquarters: An der Kaufleite 34, 90562 Kalchreuth, Bavaria, Germany.
- Departments/work areas: OPC UA SDKs, cross-platform communication frameworks, embedded C/C++, .NET/C#, QA/test automation, software certification, gateway products, OT certificate management, IoT/cloud integration, support, and consulting.
- Remote/mobile work: kununu benefits list home office and flexible working hours, but the sample size is small and exact policy should be confirmed.

## Revenue And Growth

The market is good and specialized. OPC UA remains central to industrial automation, OT/IT integration, machine communication, and Industry 4.0 interoperability. Demand is supported by industrial digitization, secure OT communication, certificate management, and cloud/IoT integration.
