# BRESSNER Technology GmbH

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - relevant industrial hardware portfolio and decent benefits, but culture reviews are only moderate.

## Applicant Summary

BRESSNER Technology GmbH is a Puchheim-based industrial hardware solution provider, system integrator, and value-added distributor. It focuses on embedded systems, industrial PCs, rugged tablets, edge AI, GPU computing, industrial IoT, medical systems, networking, and communication solutions. It has operated for more than 30 years and has been part of HIPER Global Ltd. since 2026. For applicants in industrial hardware, embedded computing, rugged systems, sales engineering, and integration, the technical scope is relevant.
