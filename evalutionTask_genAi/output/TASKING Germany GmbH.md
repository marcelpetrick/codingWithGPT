## Applicant Summary

TASKING Germany GmbH is part of TASKING, a Munich-headquartered provider of embedded software development tools for compiling, debugging, and testing safety- and security-critical applications. Its tools are used in automotive, aerospace and defense, industrial, medical, robotics, and adjacent embedded markets. For applicants interested in compilers, embedded C/C++, debuggers, RTOS/toolchain support, MISRA, ISO 26262, and safety-certified development workflows, TASKING is technically attractive.
