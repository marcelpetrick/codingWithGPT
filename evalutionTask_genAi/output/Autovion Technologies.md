**Hiring fit:** Maybe - timely software-supply-chain niche, but very early-stage and little culture data.

## Applicant Summary

Autovion Technologies is a small Germany-based startup building Autovion Ledger, a platform for open-source governance, SBOM lifecycle management, software composition analysis, supplier traceability, and EU Cyber Resilience Act readiness for embedded and automotive systems. The market need is real and timely, but the company is very small and public validation is limited.

## Market And Goals

The market is promising. Automotive, embedded, industrial, and connected-product companies face rising requirements around SBOMs, OSS license compliance, vulnerability tracking, supplier evidence, UNECE R155, and the EU Cyber Resilience Act. Autovion's goal is to replace fragmented spreadsheets/manual evidence with a system of record for software supply-chain governance.
