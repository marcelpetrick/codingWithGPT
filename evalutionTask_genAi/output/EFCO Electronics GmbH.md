# EFCO Electronics GmbH

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - relevant industrial PC and HMI portfolio, but German employer data is too small for a strong recommendation.

## Company snapshot

EFCO Electronics GmbH is the German branch of EFCO, a Taiwan-origin industrial computer company. The German office is based in Deggendorf. EFCO develops and sells embedded computer platforms, fanless industrial PCs, DIN-rail IPCs, HMI systems, panel PCs, rugged computers, IoT nodes, COM carrier boards, and automation/vision computing platforms.

For embedded applicants, the technical fit is good: industrial PCs, embedded control, HMI, machine vision hardware, industrial Ethernet, wireless modules, ruggedized platforms, long-term availability, and 24/7 industrial operation.

## Size, revenue, and growth

Public profiles describe EFCO as having about **150 employees** from a German-market perspective, with around **20 engineers** contributing to its platform development. The global company has offices in Taiwan, Germany, the USA, China, and the UK. Standalone German revenue is not publicly disclosed.

## Work culture and applicant signal

Kununu data for EFCO Electronics is positive but too small to rely on strongly: about **4.3/5** from only two reviews. It points to good communication, work-life balance, colleague cohesion, leadership, and flexible hours, but the sample is thin.

This could be a reasonable choice for applicants who want industrial PC/HMI work in a smaller German team with global product backing. Applicants should verify whether the role is engineering, sales/application support, product management, or distribution, and should ask about career path, remote work, salary, and decision authority in Germany.

## Market outlook

The outlook is stable. Industrial automation, machine vision, edge AI, rugged IPCs, IIoT, and long-lifecycle computing platforms remain relevant. Competition is high from larger embedded-computing vendors.
