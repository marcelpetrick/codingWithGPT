# Renesas Electronics

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - major embedded semiconductor employer, but Germany software management fit depends heavily on local site and business unit.

## Applicant overview

- **Locations/departments:** Global headquarters in Tokyo, operating in 30+ countries. Departments include automotive MCUs, analog/power, embedded processing, connectivity, software/tools, applications, sales, product, operations, and corporate functions.
- **Revenue/growth:** FY 2025 revenue was **JPY 1.32tn**, down 2.0%; FY 2024 was **JPY 1.35tn**, down 8.2%; FY 2023 was **JPY 1.47tn**, down 2.1%. Q4 2025 revenue rebounded 20.1% year over year.
- **Market/future:** Automotive, industrial, power-efficient embedded processors, analog/power, and IoT are strong markets, though semiconductor cyclicality remains.
- **Employee satisfaction:** Germany-specific culture data was not strong in this pass. Applicants should check local software team scope, Japanese corporate decision flow, remote policy, and manager autonomy.
- **Employees:** **21,907** in 2025, about **22,000+** careers-page scale in 2024, and roughly low-20k in prior reporting.
- **Similar companies:** Infineon, NXP, STMicroelectronics, Texas Instruments, Microchip.
