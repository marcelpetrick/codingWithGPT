# Green Hills Software

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - excellent safety-critical embedded software niche, but Germany footprint is small and management-culture signals are mixed.

## Applicant overview

- **Locations/departments:** Headquarters in Santa Barbara, California; German office listed in Bonn. Departments include RTOS, compilers, debuggers, hypervisors, safety/security certification, tools, field engineering, sales, and support.
- **Revenue/growth:** Private company; Glassdoor estimates USD 100-500m revenue and 201-500 employees. Growjo estimates about USD 257m revenue, but this is third-party modeled data.
- **Market/future:** Safety/security-critical embedded systems for avionics, defense, automotive, medical, industrial, and IoT remain high-value markets. Green Hills has strong credibility through INTEGRITY, MULTI, and certification-heavy platforms.
- **Employee satisfaction:** Glassdoor shows 3.8/5 from about 30 reviews, 67% recommendation, 77% CEO approval, strong compensation and work-life scores, but weaker senior-management and culture signals. One detailed engineering review praises technical work but criticizes management load, mentorship, and insular culture.
- **Employees:** LinkedIn size band **201-500**; visible employee count around **266** in one indexed result. Prior two-year counts not public.
- **Similar companies:** Wind River, QNX, SYSGO, Vector Informatik, Lynx Software Technologies.
