# Open Source Automation Development Lab (OSADL) eG

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - excellent industrial open-source network, but probably too small/nonprofit-like for standard engineering manager hiring.

## Applicant overview

- **Locations/departments:** German cooperative focused on industrial open source. Work includes real-time Linux, safety-critical Linux, I/O frameworks, real-time Ethernet, kernel drivers, legal/compliance support, workshops, and member collaboration.
- **Revenue/growth:** Revenue is not public. OSADL was founded in 2005 and joined the Linux Foundation in 2010, indicating long-term relevance rather than scale growth.
- **Market/future:** Industrial Linux, PREEMPT_RT, open-source compliance, safety, and automation are very strong future areas, but most jobs are likely at member companies.
- **Employee satisfaction:** No meaningful employer-review signal found. Applicants should expect a small expert/cooperative environment and validate whether roles are employment, consulting, or member collaboration.
- **Employees:** Public current/prior employee counts not reliable.
- **Similar companies:** Linux Foundation, Eclipse Foundation, OPC Foundation, EtherCAT Technology Group, CAN in Automation.
