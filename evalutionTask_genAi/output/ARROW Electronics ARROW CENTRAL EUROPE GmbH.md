# ARROW Electronics ARROW CENTRAL EUROPE GmbH

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - financially strong global employer, but Germany career/pay reviews are mixed.

## Applicant Summary

Arrow Electronics is a global technology distribution and engineering-services company. Arrow Central Europe GmbH represents the German/Central European component-distribution footprint. For applicants in embedded components, field application engineering, supplier management, sales, supply chain, or enterprise technology distribution, Arrow is a serious, stable employer with broad market access. The main caveat is culture: public reviews point to decent work-life balance and scale, but also weak career development, uneven management, and pay concerns.

## Location And Departments

- Global headquarters: Centennial, Colorado, USA.
- German/Central Europe location listed by LinkedIn: Frankfurter Strasse 211, Neu-Isenburg, Hesse, Germany.
- Departments/work areas: electronic components distribution, embedded/semiconductor sales, field application engineering, supply chain, supplier/customer management, enterprise computing solutions, cloud/AI/data-center distribution, marketing, logistics, and business development.
- Remote/mobile work: likely role-dependent. Large-company hybrid/flexible arrangements exist in some areas, but reviews suggest manager and function matter.

## Revenue And Growth

Arrow is public and publishes financials.

- 2025: sales of USD 30.9B, up 10% year over year.
- 2024: sales of USD 27.9B, down from the prior semiconductor-cycle peak.
- 2025 segment growth: Global Components sales USD 21.5B, up 8%; Global ECS sales USD 9.35B, up 18%.
- Employees: StockAnalysis lists about **22,230** employees; LinkedIn shows about **21,927** visible employees.

Interpretation: large, cyclical, but financially strong. 2025 shows recovery and renewed momentum, especially in ECS/cloud/AI/data-center activity.

## Market And Goals

Arrow operates in a strong but cyclical market: semiconductors, embedded electronics, design engineering, enterprise computing, cloud, AI, data centers, and supply-chain services. It benefits from broad technology demand but remains exposed to inventory cycles and electronics-market downturns.

## Employee Satisfaction

- Glassdoor: 3.7 score from 2,533 reviews; 68% recommendation; 53% positive business outlook.
- Indeed: 3.4 overall from more than 1,400 reviews; lower scores for advancement and management.
- kununu Germany: salary satisfaction is modest; only 39% satisfied with salary in one salary overview. Career/development is one of the weaker German factors.
- Positive themes: stable brand, global reach, decent work-life balance in some teams, good colleagues, benefits, professional structure.
- Negative themes: slow career growth, management inconsistency, layoffs/cyclical pressure, low or uneven salary increases, bureaucracy.
- Ambitious people: good for people who want broad supplier/customer exposure and enterprise-scale process; less ideal if they need fast internal mobility or high-growth compensation.

## Decision

Good company for hiring? **Maybe.** Arrow is worth considering for stable global exposure, embedded distribution, and FAE/sales/supply-chain roles. I would validate manager quality, target/commission plan, salary band, training budget, internal mobility, and how the German team is handling market-cycle pressure.
