# AUMOVIO Engineering Solutions

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - strong mobility engineering scope, but restructuring and job-security risk are significant.

## Applicant Summary

AUMOVIO Engineering Solutions is the engineering-services arm within AUMOVIO, the former Continental Automotive business. It delivers customized engineering and production solutions for automotive and adjacent industries such as rail, urban air mobility, maritime, motorsport, electrification, ADAS, connectivity, HMI, E/E architecture, simulation, and remanufacturing. The technical scope is broad and attractive, but AUMOVIO is in a difficult automotive-supplier transition with restructuring and workforce reductions.

## Location And Departments

- Engineering Solutions headquarters listed by LinkedIn: Frankfurt, Hesse, Germany.
- German locations include Frankfurt, Regensburg, Markdorf, Karben, and Ingolstadt; global locations include UK, Italy, Portugal, France, Romania, Hungary, China, Japan, India, Mexico, and USA.
- Parent AUMOVIO headquarters: Guerickestrasse 7, Frankfurt, Germany.
- Departments/work areas: brakes/chassis, engine management, hybrid/electric systems, transmission control, E/E architecture, ADAS/autonomous driving, connectivity, HMI, simulation, vehicle integration, samples/special services, production engineering, remanufacturing, railway/motorsport/non-automotive engineering.
- Remote/mobile work: Glassdoor and kununu signals mention hybrid/flexible work in some roles, but availability depends on site and function.

## Revenue And Growth

AUMOVIO is public and publishes figures; Engineering Solutions standalone revenue is not separately disclosed in the sources found.

- 2025: AUMOVIO adjusted consolidated sales of EUR 18.5B, down 5.0% from 2024; adjusted EBIT EUR 717M, up 45.4%.
- 2024: adjusted sales EUR 19.5B; LinkedIn says almost EUR 20B revenue and nearly **93,000** employees as of Dec. 31, 2024.
- 2025 workforce: AUMOVIO facts page reports around **82,000** employees globally.
- 2026 outlook: adjusted consolidated sales expected between EUR 17.0B and EUR 18.5B, with additional restructuring and up to 4,000 positions affected globally.

Interpretation: profitability improved, but revenue and headcount are under pressure. This is a restructuring turnaround, not a calm growth story.

## Market And Goals

The market is important but difficult. AUMOVIO targets software-defined vehicles, autonomous mobility, safety, connectivity, sensors, displays, braking, comfort systems, and electrification. These are future markets, but European automotive suppliers face pricing pressure, slower EV demand, Chinese competition, and OEM insourcing.

## Employee Satisfaction

- Glassdoor AUMOVIO: 3.3 from 121 reviews; 54% recommendation; 24% positive business outlook.
- kununu AUMOVIO: 3.6 from more than 2,000 reviews; 66% recommendation.
- Positive themes: work-life balance, hybrid/flexible work in some roles, friendly colleagues, modern tools in some software areas, technical learning.
- Negative themes: restructuring uncertainty, low business outlook, limited promotion opportunities, weak senior management ratings, salary/raise complaints, job-security concerns.
- Ambitious people: strong technical exposure is possible, but career progression may be constrained by reorgs and project uncertainty.
- Family friendliness: likely decent in some German teams because of work-life balance/flexible-work signals, but instability lowers the overall confidence.

## Decision

Good company for hiring? **Maybe.** It can be a strong technical move for automotive software, ADAS, E/E architecture, or systems engineering, but I would only proceed after validating the exact site's restructuring exposure, project funding, team roadmap, manager quality, and severance/history of layoffs. Avoid joining a team without a clear committed customer program.
