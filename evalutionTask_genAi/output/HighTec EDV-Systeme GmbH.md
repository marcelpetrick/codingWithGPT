# HighTec EDV-Systeme GmbH

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - strong embedded compiler/toolchain niche, but employer reviews are contradictory and should be checked carefully.

## Applicant overview

- **Locations/departments:** Headquarters in Saarbrücken. Work areas include embedded software, C/C++, Rust, LLVM, compilers, RTOS/toolchains, automotive/industrial safety, sales, support, and engineering services.
- **Revenue/growth:** Revenue is not public. SignalHire lists 100-200 employees and founded in 1982, while LinkedIn/official scale should be checked in interviews.
- **Market/future:** Safety-critical embedded compilers, Rust, LLVM, and automotive/industrial toolchains are strong future markets, especially as certified toolchains evolve.
- **Employee satisfaction:** Kununu has only two reviews and a very weak 1.5/5 score; Glassdoor has nine reviews at 4.2/5 with 73% recommendation. Treat both as small samples. Common interview checks: office policy, salary competitiveness, leadership style, and engineering autonomy.
- **Employees:** Third-party profile says **100-200**; previous two-year counts not public.
- **Similar companies:** TASKING, Green Hills Software, IAR, Lauterbach, Vector Informatik.
