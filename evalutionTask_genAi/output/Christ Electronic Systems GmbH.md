# Christ Electronic Systems GmbH

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - relevant HMI and industrial embedded products, but reviews are mixed and salary/career signals need checking.

## Company snapshot

Christ Electronic Systems GmbH is a Memmingen-based German electronics manufacturer founded in 1996. It develops and produces touch panel PCs, touch displays, industrial PCs, and customer-specific HMI solutions for industrial, medical, pharma, food technology, automotive, BDE/MES, and shopfloor applications.

For embedded applicants, the technical relevance is good: ARM and x86 platforms, industrial displays, panel PCs, PCB/hardware design, thermal/mechanical integration, custom interfaces, and rugged industrial electronics.

## Work culture and applicant signal

Kununu shows around **3.9/5** with roughly **75% recommendation** over the last two years. Positive signals include interesting products, colleague cohesion, working conditions, and some good recent reviews. Negative signals include salary, career development, communication, pressure, and management style in some reviews.

This looks like a reasonable fit for engineers who want tangible industrial hardware products in a smaller manufacturing environment. It is not a clear top employer signal because the review base is small and includes sharp negative feedback. Applicants should verify salary band, remote flexibility, leadership style, and development opportunities before accepting.

## Market outlook

The market is stable. Industrial HMI, panel PCs, rugged displays, machine operation, medical/food/pharma equipment, and embedded control interfaces remain necessary in automation. Growth is likely steady rather than explosive.
