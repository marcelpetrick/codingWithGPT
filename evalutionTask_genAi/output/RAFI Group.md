# RAFI Group

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - excellent HMI/EMS manufacturer with strong reviews, but software management fit depends on the specific role.

## Applicant overview

- **Locations/departments:** Headquarters in Berg, Baden-Wurttemberg, with eight locations in six countries. Departments include HMI components/systems, EMS, embedded electronics, production, development, quality, sales, purchasing, and corporate functions.
- **Revenue/growth:** Revenue is not public in this pass. RAFI states around **2,000** employees and more than 120 years of history; kununu company info says nearly 2,400 employees.
- **Market/future:** Human-machine interfaces, EMS, industrial controls, medical/vehicle interfaces, and electronics manufacturing are durable markets.
- **Employee satisfaction:** Kununu shows 4.5/5 from 720 reviews, strong culture, tasks, work-life balance, leadership, and 65% recent recommendation. Pay and career are solid, not exceptional.
- **Employees:** RAFI employer page says **2,000**; kununu profile says nearly **2,400**. Prior two-year counts not public.
- **Similar companies:** EAO, Schurter, Marquardt, Cherry, ZF Electronics.
