# Apollo Green Solutions

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - promising cleantech market, but early-stage startup risk and limited culture data.

## Applicant Summary

Apollo Green Solutions is a Cologne/Athens startup building AI-powered energy management through its aenergi platform. It connects PV, EV fleets, batteries, building loads, sensors, and energy-market/weather data to optimize industrial and commercial energy use. The market is attractive and the company appears active, visible, and currently hiring, but it is still very small and lacks mature public employer-review data.

## Location And Departments

- Headquarters/listed primary location: Cologne, Germany.
- Additional location: Athens, Greece.
- Departments/work areas: AI energy management, software development, hardware/sensor integration, energy consulting, battery optimization, sales/business development, customer projects, sustainability/energy strategy.
- Remote/mobile work: no clear formal policy found. Roles appear tied to Cologne/Athens and customer/project work; confirm hybrid/remote expectations in interview.

## Revenue And Growth

Apollo Green Solutions is private and does not publish revenue.

## Market And Goals

The market is strong: energy efficiency, ISO 50001 compliance, EU energy-efficiency regulation, PV/battery/EV optimization, and AI-driven energy management are all demand-supported areas. Apollo's goal is to make company energy usage smarter, more flexible, and profitable while accelerating decarbonization.
