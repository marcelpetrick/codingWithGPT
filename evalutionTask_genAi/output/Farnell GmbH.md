# Farnell GmbH

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - strong Avnet-owned electronics distributor with hybrid/career resources, but software engineering management roles are likely limited in Germany.

## Applicant overview

- **Locations/departments:** Farnell operates globally as part of Avnet. German customer-facing presence includes sales and technical support; global functions include e-commerce, supply chain, product, engineering support, purchasing, finance, and HR.
- **Revenue/growth:** Farnell is integrated into Avnet, so separate German revenue is not public. Farnell states more than 3,000 employees, 20 main offices, 13 warehouses, and more than 10 million products on demand.
- **Market/future:** Electronic component distribution is cyclical but strategically important for design, maintenance, and repair. Farnell's digital commerce and design-community model is relevant, though the best software roles may be centralized outside Germany.
- **Employee satisfaction:** Farnell's own career material highlights hybrid work, learning/development, MentorConnect, DEI, and international projects. Independent Germany-specific review signal is weaker than for Avnet overall.
- **Employees:** Farnell reports **3,000+** employees globally; previous two-year counts not separately disclosed.
- **Similar companies:** Digi-Key, Mouser Electronics, RS Components, Arrow Electronics, TME.
