# embedded data GmbH

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - attractive industrial IoT technology and positive reviews, but very small company scale limits management-role fit.

## Company snapshot

embedded data GmbH is an industrial IoT technology company based in Armsheim, Germany. Since 2017 it has developed complete IoT solutions for industrial machine and device manufacturers, covering gateway hardware selection, embedded/edge software, cloud platforms, dashboards, APIs, OTA updates, and operation.

Its david IoT platform uses standard technologies such as Docker, PostgreSQL, MQTT, REST, Modbus, OPC UA, embedded Linux, private cloud, on-premise, and public cloud options. The company emphasizes vendor independence, source-code access on request, DSGVO compliance, and Cyber Resilience Act readiness.

## Work culture and applicant signal

Kununu shows around **4.2/5** from **13 reviews**, with positive scores for work-life balance, atmosphere, communication, colleague cohesion, interesting work, career development, home office, flexible hours, and benefits. The caveat is size: in a very small company, formal career paths and engineering management roles may be limited.

This is a good technical target for hands-on senior engineers, IoT architects, or people who want broad ownership. It is less likely to be an ideal software engineering manager target unless the company is explicitly scaling.

## Market outlook

The outlook is good. Industrial IoT, retrofit, edge gateways, secure updateability, data sovereignty, and CRA-compliant connected products are relevant. The main business risk is small-company execution and customer concentration.
