- **Locations/departments:** QNX is BlackBerry's real-time OS and embedded software business. German presence is typically customer-facing/automotive/field engineering; global teams cover RTOS, hypervisor, safety certification, automotive, medical, industrial robotics, product, support, and sales.

- **Market/future:** Safety-certified RTOS, automotive, robotics, medical devices, AI-powered machines, and industrial systems are strong markets.
- **Employee satisfaction:** Public employer data is usually for BlackBerry rather than QNX Germany. Applicants should check whether the role is local leadership, field engineering, or global product engineering.
- **Employees:** QNX-specific employee counts not public; BlackBerry/QNX operates globally.
