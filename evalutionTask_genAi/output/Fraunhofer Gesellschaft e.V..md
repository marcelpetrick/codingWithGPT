# Fraunhofer Gesellschaft e.V.

**Hiring fit:** Maybe - excellent technical mission and Munich headquarters, but institute-dependent leadership and public-sector constraints matter.

## Applicant overview

- **Locations/departments:** Headquarters in Munich, with 76 institutes and research units across Germany. Departments span applied research, software, electronics, AI, mobility, security, energy, administration, transfer, and project management.
- **Revenue/growth:** Fraunhofer reported a 2024 total business volume in the multi-billion-euro range; personnel expenses rose with 3.8% permanent-staff growth and TVöD pay increases. It is stable and publicly supported, not venture-growth oriented.
- **Market/future:** Applied research in AI, embedded systems, semiconductors, cybersecurity, mobility, and energy is highly future-relevant. Roles can be technically excellent, but management scope varies sharply by institute.
- **Employee satisfaction:** Kununu shows 3.6/5 from 1,175 reviews and 65% recent recommendation. Strengths: image, interesting tasks, collegiality, work-life balance, flexible hours/home office. Risks: institute-specific leadership, top-down communication, fixed-term contracts, and slower bureaucracy.
- **Employees:** Fraunhofer describes more than **30,000** employees; exact previous two-year group totals vary by reporting date and were not extracted here.
- **Similar companies:** Max Planck Society, Helmholtz Association, DLR, Leibniz Association, TNO.
