# WAGO GmbH & Co. KG

**Hiring fit:** Yes - stable family-owned automation company with strong employer and market signals.

## Applicant Summary

WAGO GmbH & Co. KG is a global supplier of connection technology, automation technology, and interface electronics. The company is family-owned, headquartered in Minden, and has a long industrial track record since 1951. For applicants in industrial automation, electronics, embedded/control systems, PLCs, IIoT, production, product development, sales engineering, and software via M&M Software, WAGO looks like one of the stronger employers in the list.

## Location And Departments

- Headquarters: Hansastrasse 27, 32423 Minden, Germany.
- German sites: Minden and Sondershausen, with more than 4,000 employees at headquarters and around 4,400 in Germany overall.
- Global footprint: nine international production/sales locations, additional sales companies, subsidiaries including M&M Software, and representatives in over 80 countries.
- Departments/work areas: electrical interconnection, spring clamp technology, automation systems, PLC/control technology, interface electronics, industrial and building automation, energy/process technology, transportation, production, R&D, software, sales, and support.
- Remote/mobile work: review data indicates good work-life balance overall, but exact remote policy depends on role; production and lab roles are naturally more site-bound.

## Revenue And Growth

WAGO publishes key figures publicly.

- 2024: turnover of EUR 1.24B.
- 2026/current company profile: around **9,000** employees worldwide, including around **4,400** in Germany.
- Older public data: 2018 revenue EUR 932M and more than 8,500 employees, indicating long-term growth over the past years.

Interpretation: stable, established, and globally relevant. Growth is not startup-style, but the company appears financially substantial and durable.

## Market And Goals

The market is strong and durable: industrial automation, building automation, energy infrastructure, electrical interconnection, IIoT, smart connected systems, and control-cabinet automation. WAGO’s positioning as the “backbone” of smart/sustainable connected infrastructure is credible because its products sit in essential industrial and electrical systems.

## Employee Satisfaction

- kununu: 3.8 score from 609 reviews; 78% recommendation in the last two years.
- Glassdoor: 4.2 score from 49 reviews; 88% recommendation.
- Positive themes: colleague cohesion, family-like environment, modern equipment, good work-life balance, tariff-based compensation plus bonuses, stable company image.
- Weaker points: communication and leadership behavior vary by manager/team.
- Pay and benefits: kununu salary/benefits is 3.9; reviews mention above-average tariff pay and bonuses.
- Ambitious people: good fit for people seeking long-term industrial technology careers, solid training, and broad domain depth. Less ideal if someone wants startup speed or large software-company compensation.
- Family friendliness: positive overall because of work-life balance and stable family-business profile.

## Decision

Good company for hiring? **Yes.** WAGO is a strong target for applicants who want stable industrial technology, automation, electronics, and long-term engineering or product careers. I would still validate the specific manager, team culture, remote policy, and growth path, but the baseline employer signal is positive.
