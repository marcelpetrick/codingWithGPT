## Group-stage scoreline heuristics

(For your 36 group matches — see [`pool_rules.md`](pool_rules.md))

- **Big-mismatch favorite vs minnow**: pick **2–0** (modal scoreline).
- **Top-10 vs Top-10**: pick **1–1** or **2–1** to the slightly higher
  team on form.
- **Two evenly matched mid-teams**: pick **1–1**.
- **Host nation at home**: lean +0.5 goals to the host.
- **Altitude games (Mexico City / Guadalajara)**: defenders score
  fewer goals than expected; trim 0.5 goals.
