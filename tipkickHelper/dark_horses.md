# Dark Horses

Teams the market still under-rates relative to their realistic ceiling.

## 🇲🇦 Morocco — the obvious one

- **2022 semi-finalists**. Still under Walid Regragui.
- Squad: Hakimi (RB), Ziyech, Amrabat, Brahim Díaz, Eliesse Ben Seghir,
  Bono in goal.
- **Group C with Brazil** — and it's a literal QF rematch from 2022,
  which Morocco won. Real chance of finishing top of the group, which
  flips the bracket side.
- **Ceiling**: semi-final again, plausible. Final, just barely.
- **Bonus-pick value**: Morocco as a semi-finalist is a 4-point
  contrarian play if you have one slot to differentiate.

## 🇭🇷 Croatia — the eternal stayer

- Modrić's farewell tournament (he's 40). Still tactically the smartest
  midfield in the field.
- **Group L with England**. Croatia loves to ruin English summers.
- 2018 finalists, 2022 third place. **Pattern**: Croatia always over-
  performs World Cup expectations.
- **Ceiling**: semi-final realistic.
- **Bonus-pick value**: Croatia as a semi-finalist is the cleanest
  contrarian play in the field.

## 🇺🇾 Uruguay — Bielsa's tactical lab

- Conceded only 12 goals in 18 CONMEBOL qualifiers — best defense in
  the South American group.
- Squad: Núñez, Pellistri, Valverde, Araújo, Bentancur, Ugarte.
  Genuine top-12 squad.
- **Group H with Spain** — could win the group on goal difference
  and avoid a brutal R32 draw.
- **Ceiling**: quarter-final, with semi-final upside if the bracket
  opens up.

## 🇨🇴 Colombia — peak generation

- Year-on-year rising under Néstor Lorenzo. Beat Brazil twice in 2024.
- Squad: Luis Díaz, James Rodríguez (37 but still creates), Cuadrado,
  Lerma, Daniel Muñoz.
- **Group K with Portugal** — possibly the toughest 2nd-seed group.
- Bookmakers rank them ~10th overall. Could go further.
- **Bonus-pick value**: Colombia as semi-finalist is a real long shot
  but defensible.

## 🇯🇵 Japan — quietly elite now

- Qualified ruthlessly. Played 2022 at high level (beat Germany and
  Spain in groups).
- Squad: Kubo, Mitoma, Kamada, Endo, Tomiyasu (if fit), Maeda, Itō.
- **Group F with Netherlands and Sweden** — the most balanced group.
  Japan **can win it**.
- **Ceiling**: quarter-final realistic, semi-final a stretch.

## 🇦🇹 Austria — Rangnick's pressing machine

- Underrated outside Germany/Austria. Has beaten Germany in a friendly,
  ran Turkey close at Euro 2024.
- **Group J with Argentina** — solid 2nd-place candidate.
- **Ceiling**: R16, possibly QF with a friendly draw.
- More of a "spoiler" than a champion.

## 🇪🇨 Ecuador — youngest team in the field

- Average squad age ~24. Won't blow you away but qualified ahead of
  Uruguay/Colombia in CONMEBOL.
- **Group E with Germany** — can sneak 2nd if Germany over-rotate.
- **Ceiling**: R16.

## Which dark horse to pick?

**For the bonus "Who reaches the semi-finals?" pick (4 teams)**:
- 2 chalks: France, Spain
- 1 mid: Argentina or Brazil
- 1 contrarian: **Croatia** (best risk-adjusted dark horse) or
  **Morocco** (best narrative dark horse, slightly worse fit for the
  draw) or **Portugal** (if you don't treat them as chalk).
