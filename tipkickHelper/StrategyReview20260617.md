predicted (3–1 not 2–1, 4–1 not 2–0, 3–0 not 2–0, 3–1 not 2–0).

repeat pattern.

Ghana, Uzbekistan), stick with the conservative picks — these are not
tier-mismatch blow-outs at the same scale.
