# Missed Games — the cost of forgotten entries

_Compiled 17 July 2026. Post-mortem on entries we failed to submit, and what
that cost us in the standings. This is a "failure analysis," kept separate from
the strategy reviews so the lesson is easy to find next tournament._

---

## What failed

The failure was **not a bad prediction** — it was **not entering picks at all**.
In Kicktipp, a missing entry scores a guaranteed **0**, exactly like a completely
wrong pick, with no chance at the 2/3/4-point outcomes.

**The two forgotten games were the 2 missed entries in the R16 (Achtelfinale /
"Ac" round).** They are the reason our Ac haul was only **8 points** — our worst
knockout round of the tournament on a per-match basis (1.00 pts/match vs a 1.65
tournament average).

> Note: this is distinct from the ST14 (Halbfinale) disaster. There the picks
> were *entered but wrong* — `1:0` France (lost 0–2) and `1:1` England (lost 1–2).
> Both would have scored 0 whether or not we'd entered them, because both
> favourites lost. Forgetting cost us nothing there. **The forgotten-entry damage
> lives entirely in the R16.**

---

## The scoring ceiling per game

Kicktipp here pays **4 / 3 / 2 / 0** per match:

| Outcome | Points |
|---|---:|
| Exact result | 4 |
| Correct goal difference | 3 |
| Correct tendency (right winner, wrong score) | 2 |
| Miss (or **no entry**) | 0 |

**Max for one game = 4. Max for two forgotten games = 8.**

---

## What those games would *usually* have yielded (our own history)

Match points to date: **168 over 102 matches** (all 104 tournament games minus
the bronze + final still to play).

| Round | Matches | Our pts | Pts/match |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| Group stage (1–10) | 72 | 119 | 1.65 |
| R32 (Se) | 16 | 35 | 2.19 |
| R16 (Ac) | 8 | 8 | 1.00 |
| QF (Vi) | 4 | 6 | 1.50 |
| SF (Ha) | 2 | 0 | 0.00 |
| **Total** | **102** | **168** | **1.65** |

- **Mean: 1.65 pts/match.**
- **Median: ≈ 2 pts/match** — a *correct tendency* is our typical result.
  (Estimated from round-level totals; with per-match scores drawn from {0,2,3,4}
  and a 1.65 mean, the middle value sits on 2.)

**Expected value of the two forgotten games:**
- Mean-based: 2 × 1.65 ≈ **3.3 pts**
- Median-based: 2 × 2 = **4 pts**
- Absolute ceiling: **8 pts**

So on our own form we threw away roughly **3–4 points** by not entering.

## Why it matters — the podium math

Standings after ST14 (17.07):

| Pos | Total | Gap to us |
|----:|------:|--:|
| 3rd | 227 | +3 |
| **4th (us)** | **224** | — |

The ~3–4 points we'd normally have banked from the two forgotten R16 games is
**almost exactly the gap that keeps us off the podium.** The bad semifinal picks
did not cost us a place (both favourites lost regardless); **the forgotten
entries did.**

---

## The lesson (actionable)

1. **A forgotten entry = a guaranteed 0.** It is the single most avoidable way to
   lose points — worse than a bold wrong pick, which at least has upside.
2. At our median rate, **every un-entered game costs ~2 points**; two costs a
   podium place.
3. **Before every matchday lock, confirm the tips are actually saved in the app.**
   For the remaining games: bronze (18.07 ~22:59 CET) and final (19.07 ~20:59
   CET) are worth ~3–4 pts to us at median — **enter them and verify.**
