# Tournament Form Perspective — 13 July 2026

Full-tournament match logs and form reads for the four semifinalists,
compiled 13.07.2026. This is the justification of record for the ST14 picks:
**`1:0` Frankreich–Spanien** and **`1:1` England–Argentinien**.

---

## The meta-finding

At the top, chalk held — all four semifinalists were pre-tournament top-5
favourites. But **tight knockout games do not resolve in 90 minutes**: five
of eight knockout ties between two decent sides were level at 90
(GER–PAR, NED–MAR, SUI–COL, NOR–ENG, ARG–SUI). Tendency-level chalk picking
was correct all tournament; every recent points leak came from putting a
*winner's* scoreline on a *coin-flip* game. Strategy A's own templates
(`2:1` clear chalk / `1:0` tight / `1:1` even) cover this — they must be
applied per-match, not defaulted to `2:1`.
