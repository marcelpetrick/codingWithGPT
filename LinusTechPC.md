## ⚠️ Missing Component: RAM

**The original LTT parts list does not include RAM — the system will not boot without it.**

The TRX50 platform requires **DDR5 RDIMM (ECC Registered)** — standard desktop DDR5 UDIMM will not work.

## Notes

- **GPU**: LTT link is for the Intel Arc B580 Limited Edition (reference card). Board partner cards (Sparkle, ASRock) are available cheaper from ~€258 DE.
- **SSD**: A version with integrated heatsink (MZ-VAP2T0GW) is available overall from ~€292 — may be more suitable for a Threadripper build with high sustained I/O.
- **Case**: Fractal Design Torrent natively supports E-ATX; Black Solid is the cheapest color variant.
- **RAM platform note**: TRX50 uses 4 DIMM slots in quad-channel; fully populating all 4 slots is required for peak memory bandwidth on the 9960X.
